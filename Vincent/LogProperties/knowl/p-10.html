Let \(f(x) = b^{x}\) be an exponential function (\(b > 0\text{,}\) \(b\neq 1\)) and let \(u\) and \(w\) be real numbers.

  • Product Rule: \(f(u+w) = f(u) f(w)\text{.}\) In other words, \(b^{u+w} = b^{u} b^{w}\)

  • Quotient Rule: \(f(u-w) = \dfrac{f(u)}{f(w)}\text{.}\) In other words, \(b^{u-w} = \dfrac{b^{u}}{b^{w}}\)

  • Power Rule: \(\left(f(u)\right)^w = f(uw)\text{.}\) In other words, \(\left(b^{u}\right)^{w} = b^{uw}\)
