To expand \(\log_{2}\left(\frac{8}{x}\right)\text{,}\) we use the Quotient Rule identifying \(u = 8\) and \(w=x\) and simplify.

\begin{align*} \log_{2}\left(\dfrac{8}{x}\right) \amp = \log_{2}(8) - \log_{2}(x) \tag{Quotient Rule}\\ \amp = 3 - \log_{2}(x) \tag{Since $2^{3} = 8$}\\ \amp = - \log_{2}(x) + 3 \end{align*}
