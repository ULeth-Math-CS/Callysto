Let \(z = 2 + 3i\) and \(w = 1-2i\text{.}\) Then

\begin{equation*} z + w = (2 + 3i) + (1 - 2i) = 3 + i \end{equation*}

and

\begin{equation*} z w = (2 + 3i)(1 - 2i ) = 8 - i. \end{equation*}

Also,

\begin{align*} z^{-1} & = \frac{2}{13} - \frac{3}{13}i\\ |z| & = \sqrt{13}\\ \overline{z} & = 2-3i. \end{align*}
